  first appearence of multiple Fatalities - as well as additional, non-lethal
  finishing moves - in the series. In addition to the Fatalities of its
  predecessor, MKII offers 'Babalities' (turning a defeated opponent into a crying
  baby), 'Friendships' (a non-malicious interaction, such as dancing or giving a
  gift to the defeated opponent) and stage-specific Fatalities (the winner
  uppercutting their opponent into an abyss below, spikes above, or a pool of acid
  in the background).
  .
  Each character still shares generic attributes - speed, power, jump height and
  airtime - albeit with differing hit boxes. The sequel drops the points system of
  its prequel in favour of a consecutive win tally.
